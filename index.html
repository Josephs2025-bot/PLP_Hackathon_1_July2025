About

Who Am I?

Hello! I’m a visionary young Kenyan with a deep passion for technology; particularly software development. I’m currently pursuing my studies as a Clinical Officer, and I’m driven by the desire to bridge the gap between healthcare and technology. My goal is to optimize healthcare service delivery and inspire innovative solutions that transform lives. I believe in empowering young minds to explore the possibilities of tech in health. Let’s connect and turn this vision into reality.

What Drives Me

I’m driven by the belief that technology holds the power to solve real-world problems, especially in the healthcare space. Growing up in Kenya, I’ve witnessed the challenges that both patients and providers face, and this fuels my commitment to create impactful solutions. Every line of code I write is guided by a desire to make healthcare smarter, faster, and more accessible. I’m also passionate about encouraging other young people to dream big, explore tech, and use it as a tool for positive change.

My Mission

My mission is to be at the forefront of merging healthcare and technology to improve lives. I aim to build innovative tools that simplify service delivery, empower healthcare workers, and enhance patient care. Through continuous learning and collaboration, I’m working toward a future where young minds in Africa are not just users of technology, but creators and changemakers. Every project I take on is a step toward that goal.
